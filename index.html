How to Play

Monster Survivors features intuitive controls designed to keep you focused on the action:

  • W Move Up
  • A Move Left
  • S Move Down
  • D Move Right
  • SPACE Special Ability

On mobile devices, use the on-screen controls to navigate and unleash your special abilities with a simple tap.

Game Tips

To maximize your survival time in Monster Survivors:

- Prioritize movement and positioning to avoid getting surrounded

- Collect experience orbs to level up quickly

- Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle

- Watch for special power-ups that can turn the tide of battle

- Learn enemy patterns to anticipate their movements
